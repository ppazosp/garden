tags:

AI Agents are systems that use LLMs to autonomously plan, reason, and take actions to accomplish goals.

Key components:
- **Planning** — breaking tasks into steps
- **Tool use** — interacting with external systems
- **Memory** — retaining context across interactions
- **Reflection** — evaluating and correcting own output
